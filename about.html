What is KORAVEL?

KORAVEL is a combination of Korea and Travel.
We provide information about travel to Korea for foreigners traveling to Korea.



Why did you make the KORAVEL?

KORAVEL was created to help foreigners who want to travel to Korea but lack information.
We propose a city-oriented travel course, not a fragmentary travel destination you can find through Google or YouTube.



What information KORAVEL have?

KORAVEL provides information on city-oriented travel destinations such as Seoul and Busan, and in detail, information on tourist attractions, travel destinations, and famous restaurants.
We are favorable to foreign travelers, and if you have any related inquiries, please feel free to contact us on the website.
We are willing to help you with your trip.
Enjoy your trip, enjoy Korea!
